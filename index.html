Mission

Advance biological understanding by openly dissecting and demystifying the latest computational and AI tools, turning hard-won technical insights into shared, actionable knowledge.

A globally connected community of scientists whose collective literacy in computational biology rises so quickly that yesterday’s cutting-edge methods become today’s standard practices, leading to breakthrough science.

Overview

OpenBioData.ai Brown Bag is a monthly virtual discussion where researchers dissect the newest computational tools propelling biological discovery. Anyone from research or tech is welcome; hype is not.

Format

  • Opening (1–2 min)
  • Per speaker (~10–15 min)
  • Lightning panel (5 min)
  • Wrap-up (1 min)

Total time: 45 minutes.

Ground Rules

  • Tools discussed must have been used within the past six months.
  • No selling or product pitches—violators receive playful heckling.
  • Show whatever communicates best: demos, code, benchmarks, anecdotes.
  • Sessions start fully moderated; adjustments come as community grows.
